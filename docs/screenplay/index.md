## Format notes

Fountain is a markdown-like plain-text screenplay format that renders cleanly to PDF, FDX (Final Draft), and HTML.

Page-count rule of thumb: **1 page ≈ 1 minute of screen time**. The 88,092-word novel would render to ~150-180 screenplay pages — feature-length-and-a-half, or a 6-episode limited series at 30min per.

Adaptation choices in the pilot:
- **Three parallel cold-open sequences** instead of three full chapters — establishes three POVs in ~12 pages, sets up cross-cutting structure for the rest.
- **V.O. (voice-over)** for interior thoughts that the prose carries via free indirect — preserves Andrés's dread and Itzcoatl's awe without losing pace.
- **No spoken Latin/Nahuatl/Mayan translations on screen** — italicized in dialogue only; subtitles in production.
- **The hum is sound design, not dialogue** — every scene with a Reliquary or a Great Engine carries a low-frequency pressure cue.

# Brief Seed

## Premise

A historical-fiction setting in which the Spanish conquest is transfigured
by reliquary-bound mecha on one side and god-engines on the other. The
narrative covers 1519 through 1521.

## The Metaphysics (Lock This First)

This section is the engine-rules core. It is a metaphysics-flavored section
and should be routed to the metaphysics axis when chunked. Pilots bind to
relics; relics are saints bound to vessels; vessels are mecha.

## Engagement Doctrine (What Battles Look Like)

Battles are ritualized. Each engagement begins with invocations. Combat
resolution is engine-rule dependent and drives narrative pacing.

## Historical Framework (Condensed)

The Spanish expedition departs Cuba in early 1519. By August they are at
Cempoala. By November they have entered Tenochtitlan. The two-thirds
revelation occurs in mid-1520. Climax is August 1521.

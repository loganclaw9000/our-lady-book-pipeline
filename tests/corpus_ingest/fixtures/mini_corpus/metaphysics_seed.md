# Metaphysics Seed

## Engine Rules

The first rule of the engine is that it must be a relic-bound vessel. The
second rule is that pilots synchronize with the relic's nature. The third
rule is that combat is ritual combat, not mechanized warfare.

## Reliquary Doctrine

Every reliquary mecha is named for its contained saint. The Spanish church
maintains the master list of approved relics. Unapproved relics may still
be bound but their pilots risk excommunication.

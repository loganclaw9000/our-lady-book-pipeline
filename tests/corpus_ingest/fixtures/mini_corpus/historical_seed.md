## Cempoala

The Totonacs of Cempoala greeted the Spanish on the coast in July 1519. They
had recently thrown off Mexica suzerainty. The fat cacique of Cempoala became
Cortes' first major native ally.

## Cholula

The massacre at Cholula occurred in October 1519. Historians disagree on the
triggering cause. Some sources cite a planned ambush; others view it as a
demonstration of Spanish intent.
